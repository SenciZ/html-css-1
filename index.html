Ferrari 360 Modena

The Ferrari 360 Modena is a two-seater coupe powered by a 3.6L V8 mid-engine that puts out 400 hp at 8,500 rpm and 275 lb-ft of torque at 4,750 rpm. That engine is paired with either a six-speed manual or F1 automated manual gearbox with paddle shifters, reaching a top speed of 183 mph.
